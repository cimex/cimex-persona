What's this all about

No matter how well you develop, without content you cannot gauge the quality of your work. Interactive sites requires content and users, hacking and wacking with loads of "Lorem Ipsom" and "test@mycompany.com" will only take you so far. Cimex Persona is a tool to help you produce meaningful user persona data to increase your project's productivity during early development phase.
